# Momentum AI Helper — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Project Overview

Momentum AI Helper is an AI-powered productivity companion that helps users accomplish their goals before deadlines through intelligent planning, adaptive scheduling, and proactive assistance.

Unlike traditional productivity applications that only remind users about tasks, Momentum AI Helper actively helps users understand, organize, prioritize, and complete their work.

The application is designed to function as an AI Executive Assistant rather than a traditional To-Do List.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many students, professionals, freelancers, and entrepreneurs miss important deadlines because they:

- Don't know where to start.
- Underestimate the time required.
- Struggle to prioritize work.
- Lose track of progress.
- Ignore reminder notifications.
- Fail to adjust their schedule when unexpected events occur.

Existing productivity tools focus primarily on reminders instead of helping users successfully complete their work.

Momentum AI Helper addresses this problem by using Artificial Intelligence to guide users throughout the entire execution process.

---

# 3. Product Vision

Our vision is to build an intelligent productivity companion that helps users:

- Plan better.
- Work smarter.
- Reduce procrastination.
- Improve consistency.
- Complete goals before deadlines.

The AI should proactively assist users instead of waiting for user instructions.

# 8. User Journey

A typical user journey:

1. User signs in.
2. User creates a goal.
3. AI asks a few questions.
4. AI breaks the goal into tasks.
5. AI estimates effort.
6. AI generates a personalized schedule.
7. User starts working.
8. AI tracks progress.
9. AI adjusts the schedule if necessary.
10. User completes the goal.
11. AI generates a productivity summary.

# 15. Product Philosophy

Momentum AI Helper is not another reminder application.

It is an AI Productivity Companion that continuously helps users answer four questions:

- What should I work on next?
- Can I still meet my deadline?
- What should change in my schedule?
- How can I complete my work with less stress and better focus?

Every feature developed for the application should support this philosophy.
